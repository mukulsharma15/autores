# Provenance: Conditional Diffusion Models for Post-Digitization ECG Refinement

## Workflow Context
- **Trigger:** User requested a deep exploration of the provided literature concerning ECG digitization, Diffusion Models, and deep learning, building on the thesis concept of refining ECG-Image-Kit extractions.
- **Tools Used:** 
  - `alpha_search` (Semantic & Keyword queries across arXiv and general literature)
  - `alpha_get_paper` (Full-text retrieval of specific arXiv IDs)
  - `fetch_content` (Crossref API evaluation of DOIs)

## Sources Consulted

1. **PTB-IMAGE: A SCANNED PAPER ECG DATASET FOR DIGITIZATION AND IMAGE-BASED DIAGNOSIS**
   - **Authors:** Cuong V. Nguyen, et al.
   - **Date:** Feb 2025
   - **Identifier:** [arXiv:2502.14909](https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.14909)
   - **Role:** Provided the definitive quantitative baseline (0.01 dB SNR) proving that optical extraction fails to capture clean biological signals, serving as the primary justification for the necessity of post-digitization generative refinement.

2. **COMPARING DEEP NEURAL NETWORK FOR MULTI-LABEL ECG DIAGNOSIS FROM SCANNED ECG**
   - **Authors:** Cuong V. Nguyen, et al.
   - **Date:** Feb 2025
   - **Identifier:** [arXiv:2502.14909v2] (Associated with above)
   - **Role:** Highlighted the shift towards diagnosing directly from images/digitized traces and the vulnerability of deep classifiers to printing/scanning artifacts.

3. **ECG-Image-Kit: A Synthetic Image Generation Toolbox...**
   - **Authors:** Shivashankara, et al.
   - **Date:** 2024
   - **Identifier:** [arXiv:2307.01946](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.01946)
   - **Role:** The foundational repository creating the artifacts (staircasing, spikes) that the thesis aims to solve.

4. **DeScoD-ECG: Deep Score-Based Diffusion Model for ECG Baseline Wander and Noise Removal**
   - **Authors:** Li, et al.
   - **Date:** Sept 2024
   - **Identifier:** [arXiv:2208.00542](https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.00542)
   - **Role:** Proved the viability of conditional 1D diffusion for extreme artifact removal in ECGs, contributing the multi-shot averaging inference technique.

5. **TFCDiff: Robust ECG Denoising via Time-Frequency Complementary Diffusion**
   - **Authors:** Li, et al.
   - **Date:** Nov 2025
   - **Identifier:** [arXiv:2511.16627](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.16627)
   - **Role:** Introduced the concept of running diffusion in the Discrete Cosine Transform (DCT) domain, directly targeting high-frequency artifacts (such as those generated by pixel staircasing).

6. **SLPM: a lightweight deep learning model for end-to-end paper ECG digitization**
   - **Date:** 2024
   - **Identifier:** [DOI: 10.1088/1361-6579/ae3fe5](https://doi.org/10.1088/1361-6579/ae3fe5)
   - **Role:** Established that the community is seeking heavy deep learning alternatives to standard heuristic computer vision extraction.

7. **Deep Generative Models: The winning key for large and easily accessible ECG datasets?**
   - **Date:** 2023
   - **Identifier:** [DOI: 10.1016/j.compbiomed.2023.107655](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compbiomed.2023.107655)
   - **Role:** Scoping review affirming Generative Models as the paradigm shift for ECG data curation.

8. **Synthetic ECG signals generation: A scoping review**
   - **Date:** 2024
   - **Identifier:** [DOI: 10.1016/j.compbiomed.2024.109453](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.compbiomed.2024.109453)
   - **Role:** Corroborated the shift from GANs/VAEs to Diffusion Models in the biomedical space.

9. **MSECG: Incorporating Mamba for Robust and Efficient ECG Super-Resolution**
   - **Date:** Dec 2024
   - **Identifier:** [arXiv:2412.04861](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.04861)
   - **Role:** Provided the conceptual leap to treat pixelated ECGs as a "Super-Resolution" problem, leveraging the Mamba (Selective State Space Model) architecture.

10. **CSDI: Conditional Score-based Diffusion Models for Probabilistic Time Series Imputation**
    - **Authors:** Tashiro, et al.
    - **Date:** 2021
    - **Identifier:** [arXiv:2107.03502](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.03502)
    - **Role:** Foundational mechanism for treating gaps in the ECG (caused by OCR text removal) as a structured imputation task via score-based diffusion.
