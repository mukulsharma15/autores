### The Full Logic Chain of Your Thesis:

 1. Raw PTB-XL Data: The perfect mathematical heartbeat (Clean Y).
 2. ECG-Image-Kit: Takes the perfect math and draws it on messy paper (.png).
 3. Baseline Digitizer: Looks at the messy paper (.png) and tries to read the math back out. It struggles because of wrinkles/grid lines, and outputs a noisy mathematical heartbeat (Noisy X).
 4. Your Diffusion Model: Takes Noisy X, learns to map it back to Clean Y, and fixes the digitizer's mistakes.
